HOW TO BECOME

  • RUSH

    Rush is the best way to get your name known around the chapter, and it also lets you get to know the brothers.

  • ACQUIRE BID

    If we believe that you strongly represent our motto and values, we will extend a bid to join the brotherhood.

  • BECOME A NEW MEMBER

    After accepting the bid and going through a 5-week New Member period, you will be initiated as a brother of Alpha Sigma Phi.

What we are looking for

Alpha Sigma Phi exemplifies men who strive to better themselves and those around them. We are looking for those who share that same vision.

What You Get

By joining Alpha Sigma Phi, you will be joining a supportive brotherhood devoted to helping you improve through our five values: Silence, Charity, Purity, Honor, and Patriotism.
